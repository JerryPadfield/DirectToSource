My Community: Direct to the Source

Source FM is always looking for new voices to have their say on the airwaves. This web page is an experiment in getting your voices direct onto the Source FM. Please record your thoughts around the subject "My Community"

This web page uses experimental internet technologies and so requires a modern, standards-compliant browser such as Chrome, Firefox, Opera. Other browsers will probably not work (sorry...), but you can still upload a file you've already recorded.

Record your audio below and upload to the Source. Press the record button to begin your recording, and stop to finish. Listen back with the Play button. When you are happy fill in your name and email details and click Submit to send your recording to the Source FM. Recordings will be edited and compiled into a show for broadcast on the Source. By submitting your audio you give Source FM the right to broadcast it. You retain the copyright. You can download your recording (in Webm format) using the link which will appear. If you change your mind and no longer want us to use your recording you can contact us at the source and we will delete your recording.

Ideas for recordings:

  • Information about Falmouth/Penryn
  • Your community in Falmouth/Penryn
  • What are the good things and bad things about living in Falmouth/Penryn
  • Messages to people you know
  • Moved away? What do you miss about the area?
  • Recordings of places in Falmouth/Penryn (you could record the sea on Gyllyngvase, the traffic on the Moor, the birds in your back garden, or the sound of a Sunday morning lie-in).
  • Got a favourite piece of music that reminds you of the area or a dedication?
